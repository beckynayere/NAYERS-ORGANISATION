NAYER'S CARE

Nayer's organisation is basically an organisation reaching out to the needy children to help them better their education and their living standards.We reach out to the poor kids providing them with stationeries and improving the hygiene status of schools.We also fight jigger infestaion thus we give them shoes.Also we educate and sensitize these needy children of various educational opportunties like scholarships and providing mentorship.
We also focus on the disabled needy kids and try to provide them with what they require in their education like we tend to buy braille machine for the needy and also wheel chair for those who cant walk on the own.

Our Vision.
Our vision is to see that every child is able to access education and better their lives with that of the society at large.
Our Mission.
We work to see that every child accesses better education and live a better life.
CORE VALUES
Care,commitment,respect for life and children rights

Our Objectives.

    The main objective states in broad terms the purpose for which the organisation is formed.The main objective has been stated thus:
  • 1To see all children and young persons leading a happy and fullfilling life.
  • 2To promote and secure the rights of children and young persons in order for then to realise their dreams.
  • 3To establish education fund mechanism to facilitate the intellectual capacity,equiping the society with the relevant knowledge.
  • 4To protect and promote the best interests and welfare of children and young persons and enable them to overcome social problems through economic development of individuals,families and communities
  • 5To advocate for and influence the public understanding of children basic and human rights and for the government budgetary allocation for children especiallyfor those in need of alternative family care eg Orphans.
